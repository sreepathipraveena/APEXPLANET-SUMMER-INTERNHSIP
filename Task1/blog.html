Quick Travel Tips

  • Copy Passports: Keep digital backups of your passport, visa, and insurance cards in cloud storage.
  • Local Phrases: Spend 10 minutes learning basic phrases (please, thank you, hello) of your host country.
  • Compact Power Bank: Always carry a lightweight external battery in your day-pack.
  • Cash is King: Keep minor local currency cash bills for rural markets and transport tipping.
